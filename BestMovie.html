Oz the Great and Powerful

Movie Information

Why I like the movie

This is my favorite movie because I love the storyline. I like how this movie is a spin on the classic The Wizard of Oz. The movie takes place in a more modern time which I like but it still has a lot of the characters that I love from the Wizard of Oz. It also is my favorite because this movie also has a lot of surprises in store that aren't in the Wizard of Oz.

Favorite Actress

My favorite actress the movie is Mila Kunis. She does a really good job of playing both good and evil. At the beginning of the movie, she is a good witch and as the movie progresses, she turns evil. She does a great job changing the characters personality throughout the movie.
